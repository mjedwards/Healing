My Story:

Sandra Hightower- Lewis, L.M.T and Nationally certified (NCTMB)

Certification Number: MA46719

Sandra studied at Margate School of Massage Institute in 2005 and successfully attained her certification with the National Board for Therapeutic Massage and Bodywork and Florida state license. She perfected her techniques through workshops and seminars in related fields such as Mind Body and Healing Connection, Swe-thai , Myokinesthetic System, Humanics Body Work, Reflexology, Neuromuscular and Sports massage. In 2004 she was inspired to become a Massage Therapist while working in the nursing field with a terminally ill patient. The patient was in excruciating pain and asked Sandra to give her a back massage, which she had no experience in at the time. The patient told all her friends and family members that “Sandra is not only the best Massage Therapist I ever had, but she also has healing hands” and then advised her to pursue a career in Massage Therapy, since then it has become her passion. Sandra is an excellent therapist who possesses a lot of positive energy. She always modifies a treatment plan that will best fit her client’s needs, she strongly believes in massage as a viable modality for enhancing overall health and well-being.
